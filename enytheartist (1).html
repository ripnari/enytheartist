La Historia / The Story

De Colombia
a Queens

ENY The Artist raps, writes, and streams out of Jackson Heights, Queens — a few blocks from the street sign that reads Calle Colombia Way. Colombian blood, New York bars, and a sound he calls The New Vintage: boom-bap bones, present-day pen.

Two full-length albums. A Funk Flex premiere on Hot 97. A first-ever Colombian feature on BlockWork TV. A headline set at the Calle Colombia Festival. And a live stream where the music gets made in front of you, every week.

Hip hop artist. Songwriter. Future Grammy award winner. Hecho en Queens. Sangre colombiana.

More story coming soon. For now — press play.
